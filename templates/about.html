About Shatsheeri

Elegant & Timeless Beauty

Our Story

সাতঁসিড়ি – Shatsheeri is an online jewelry brand that presents a beautiful, elegant, and stylish collection of jewelry for women. Our goal is to create and deliver jewelry that blends traditional beauty with modern fashion.

In today's world, women want to express their personality and style through jewelry not only on special occasions but also in daily life. Keeping this in mind, Shatsheeri is working with various jewelry collections so that every woman can create a unique style according to her preference.

Every piece of jewelry we create tells a story of craftsmanship, tradition, and contemporary elegance. We believe that jewelry is not just an accessory but an expression of one's identity.
